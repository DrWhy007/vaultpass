VaultPass Safety & Privacy

How we protect your passwords and privacy

Your Security, Our Priority

VaultPass is a 100% client-side password generator designed with your privacy as the top priority. Unlike many online tools, we've built VaultPass to work entirely within your browser - no data is ever sent to our servers or any third parties.

How It Works

Complete Client-Side Operation

  • All password generation happens directly in your browser
  • Your passwords are never transmitted over the internet
  • No tracking, analytics, or data collection

Where Your Data Lives

  • Generated passwords only exist on your current device
  • Saved passwords use your browser's localStorage
  • No databases or cloud storage used

Zero Knowledge Design

  • We cannot access your passwords
  • No account system - nothing to hack
  • Even we can't see what you generate

Privacy Protections

No Tracking

We don't use cookies, analytics, or any tracking scripts

No Backdoors

The code is open for inspection on GitHub

No Data Collection

We don't know or want to know your passwords

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my saved passwords stored?

Exclusively in your browser's local storage. They never leave your device.

What happens if I clear my browser data?

Like all locally stored data, your vault would be cleared. We recommend exporting important passwords.

Can you recover my passwords if I lose them?

No. Since we never receive your data, recovery is impossible by design.

Is this really secure?

Yes! By keeping everything client-side, we eliminate server-based vulnerabilities.

Open & Transparent

We believe in transparency. The complete source code is available on GitHub for anyone to inspect.
